Vegan Brownies

Description:

These vegan brownies are made with cocoa powder for a rich and gooey chocolaty treat.

If you prefer brownies that are a little more solid, you can bake the brownies for longer than the recommended time. Great for people with egg or dairy allergies, too!

Ingredientes:

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Cocoa powder
  • Baking powder
  • Salt
  • Water
  • Oil
  • Vanilla

Steps:

  1. Whisk the dry ingredients in a large bowl.
  2. Add the wet ingredients and stir until the batter is blended.
  3. Pour the batter into a prepared baking dish and spread evenly.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven until the top is no longer shiny and the center feels just set.
  5. Let the brownies cool before cutting them into squares.
